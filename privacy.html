Privacy Policy

Eight IT is a technology consultancy. This policy explains what information we collect when you use eightit.com and how we use it.

Information you give us

When you submit the contact form on this site, we collect your name, email address, and the message you write. We use this information to respond to your inquiry. We don't share it with third parties or add you to marketing lists.

Remote management software

If you're an Eight IT client and you've installed our remote management agent, that software connects your device to our management platform for the purpose of providing IT support. Its use is governed by your service agreement with Eight IT.

Information collected automatically

We don't use analytics software or tracking cookies on this site.

Our hosting provider, Cloudflare, processes web requests as part of delivering the site. Cloudflare may log request metadata such as IP addresses as part of their standard infrastructure operation. See Cloudflare's privacy policy for details.

This site loads the Inter typeface from Google Fonts. When you visit, your browser makes a request to Google's font servers, which may include your IP address. See Google's privacy policy for details.

Data retention

Contact form submissions are retained only as long as needed to respond to your inquiry.
