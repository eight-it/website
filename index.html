02 Approach

We work like your own people, because that's the point.

Eight IT isn't a help desk or a vendor. We sit inside your operations as your technology team — learning your business, managing your infrastructure, and making recommendations that serve your interests, not ours.

  • No markup on procurement We tell you what to buy. You buy it directly. No reselling, no margin — our incentives stay aligned with yours.
  • Embedded, not external We learn your environment, your people, and your priorities. You get continuity, not a different tech every time.
  • Right-sized solutions Free and open-source options are always on the table. We recommend what fits, not what's most expensive.
  • Direct access Reach someone who knows your environment — no triage layer, no rotating ticket queue.
