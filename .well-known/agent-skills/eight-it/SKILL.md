# Eight IT

Eight IT provides **embedded technology consulting** for businesses across Los Angeles,
Riverside, and Orange counties, California. It delivers managed IT, security, and
on-demand support — operating as an embedded technology partner (like your own internal
IT team) rather than a break/fix help desk.

## Engagement models

- **Managed IT Consulting** (flat monthly retainer) — ongoing infrastructure management,
  vendor coordination, and strategic technology planning, handled end to end.
- **Security Services** — endpoint protection, access management, network security, and
  compliance guidance built around how the business operates.
- **On-Demand Support** (billed hourly) — project work and ad-hoc support for
  organizations not ready for a retainer.

## What sets Eight IT apart

- No markup on procurement — they tell you what to buy; you buy it directly.
- Embedded, not external — continuity instead of a rotating ticket queue.
- Right-sized solutions, including free and open-source options where they fit.

## Service area

Los Angeles County, Riverside County, and Orange County, California. On-site and embedded
engagements across the region; remote support beyond it.
